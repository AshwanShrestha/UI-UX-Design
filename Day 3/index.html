Family Values Family is essential in Nepali tradition, emphasizing patriarchal joint families. Marriages are often arranged with a very low divorce rate.
Child Marriages Common, especially in rural areas, with many women married before age 18.
Nepali Festivals Major festivals include Dashain, Tihar, Teej, Chhath, Maghi, Sakela, Holi, and the Nepali New Year.
Gadhimai Festival A Hindu festival involving the large-scale sacrifice of animals to honor goddess Gadhimai.
Dashain The biggest Hindu religious festival, celebrating with offerings of blood to appease gods.
